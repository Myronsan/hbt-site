About Hotel BT

In 1792 the first publicly held hotel, called the City Hotel, opened in NYC. The first hotel in the present sense of the word, the Tremont House, opened in Boston in 1829. It was a quite remarkable place, that set many “firsts” for the industry.

Most notably it was the first hotel with indoor plumbing and indoor toilets and baths. It was also the pioneer in terms of reception as well as everyone’s favourite – free soap.
